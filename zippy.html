ZIPPY

Played by Sarah

The egg experiment in fantasy Russia. Zippy is the only success. Intended to replace dolls but make them better. There was a kobold which experienced failure to thrive. One day lab was empty so they just left. Went to orphanage, got jobs in lab district, fled the country eventually and went to school. Learned of dolls and became obsessed with them as a parallel of them. Trying to build a vessel for little friend, figure themself out. Got a job in doll production, learned about dolls and their sapience, published their research under their fake name. The revolt happened and they left, abandoned the name so as not to get in trouble with the law. Spent some time back at school to hide, have been planning how to make their own critters.

EUDORA

Pretty Princess

Eudora is the nation's darling, known for both her kindness and her somewhat cooky behavior. In truth, she thrives off the discomfort of others, and seeks to subtly sew despair into the hearts of her people. She has long, honey blonde hair, gently curled and pulled back into a half-updo. She has warm brown doe-eyes, which she accents with colorful eyeshadow. She's truly a natural beauty.
Eudora is just a sweet little gal with no malicious intentions at all!

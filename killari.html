KILLARI

Played by Lisbeth

I was a problem child, hot-headed and often losing control of my lightning powers which served as another reminder of my mother’s affair. I was never who the clan wanted me to be. I didn’t like farming and was only a half-decent druid; instead, I preferred crafting and wished to join an artisan's guild. Though my mother was disappointed, she and my adoptive father agreed to let me leave for Ingtin where I joined a guild that sold items from the Mithral mines to Oriona and Tarias.

DOROTHY

The One to Blame

Daughter of a doll tycoon billionaire, looking to ease tensions between dolls and humans by offering them her service, childhood friend of Casper. Is running away from her arranged marriage to a much older man in Taraias. Emotionally disconnected and quiet, but kind.
She's a young woman in her early 20s, with long auburn hair in an intricate hairstyle, and shiny olive green eyes. Her face is so symmetrical it seems unreal. DC 18 Perception: One of her eyes is a tad bit bigger than the other.

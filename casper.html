CASPER

Ousted Nobleman

Technically the Lord of the Engelkil region, his father died during the doll revolt when he was just a child, returning to try to get back and important family heirloom (is actually a doll, somehow doesn’t know it). His mother and father are both dead, and he believes his brother to be dead as well.
He's a young man, probably in his early 20s, with dark brown hair and eyes that seem to shift from dark blue to light green. DC 18 Perception: Looking closely at his face, it seems to be perfectly symmetrical. Despite how poor morale may be, his pallor never changes from a healthy rosy color.
